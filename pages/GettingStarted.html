Getting Started

Setting Up a New Event in Cleus

  1. Go to: myab.co and login with your username and password
  2. Click 'New' on the top right hand corner of the site.
  3. Search for the correct organization in the 'Organization Name' box.
  4. Open up TrueOps to fillout all of the important information for the event.
  5. Upload a event banner.
  6. Upload the organizations logo.
  7. Create a keyword.
  8. Make sure the Pay Now button is switched to 'Yes'.
  9. Click 'Save'.
